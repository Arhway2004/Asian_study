Overview of Communication in Taiwan

Taiwan has a highly developed communication system, with modern technology enabling seamless interactions. Traditional face-to-face communication remains important, but digital platforms like LINE, Facebook, and YouTube have become dominant in daily conversations.

Common Communication Methods

1. Languages Spoken

The primary language spoken in Taiwan is **Mandarin Chinese**, but **Hokkien (Taiwanese), Hakka, and Indigenous languages** are also widely used.

2. Digital Communication

With **one of the highest internet penetration rates** in the world, most people in Taiwan use smartphones and social media apps for communication.

3. Popular Messaging Apps

  • **LINE** – The most popular messaging app in Taiwan.
  • **Facebook Messenger** – Commonly used for casual and business conversations.
  • **WeChat** – Used by people with connections to China.
